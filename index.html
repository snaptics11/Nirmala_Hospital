World - Class Patients Facilities Designed For You

Since 25 years, Nirmala Hospital has been providing quality health care for the people in all their diverse needs. People trust us because of the strong relationships we have built with them over the years. Nirmala Hospital has become a center of excellence, majorly for obstetric and Gynaecology, Orthopaedics, and other specialties including general medicine, general surgery, and pediatrics.

Nirmala Hospital has been providing world-class health services at an affordable cost. There is never a dull moment at Nirmala Hospital. Each day, the hospital receives many walk-ins, emergency cases, and in-patients. When it comes to patient care, we score a perfect 10. We maintain uncompromised standards in patient care and diagnosis.

  • ✔️ Expert medical professionals dedicated to your health
